These service providers are obligated to keep your information confidential and are not authorized to use it for any other purpose.

We may also disclose your information if we are required to do so by law or if we believe that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no data transmission over the internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the security of your information transmitted to our website.
